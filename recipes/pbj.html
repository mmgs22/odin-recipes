PB&J

Description

Just like mom made back in the day! This peanut butter & jelly sandwich is an elevated take on the classic lunchtime bite that will fill you with nostalgia, fun, and a touch of sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread (per sandwich)
  • 1 jar of spreadable peanut butter (crunchy for added texture)
  • 1 jar of strawberry preserves (can substitute fruit flavors)

Steps

  1. On inside of one slice of bread, spread thin layer of peanut butter with butter knife.
  2. With cleaned knife, spread perserves generously across inside of opposite slice of bread.
  3. Combine insides of slices and enjoy!
