Grilled Cheese

Description

Nothing beats a classic grilled cheese. Until now. This take on the all-American classic lights up your tastebuds with ooey gooey flavor in every bite. We won't tell Mom!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread (per sandwich)
  • 3-4 tbsp salted butter, softened
  • 4 slices muenster cheese
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Spread softened butter on either side of bread slices.
  2. Add 2 slices of muenster cheese and 1 slice cheddar to each bread slice.
  3. Heat large nonstick pan just below medium heat and melt leftover butter.
  4. Add sandwich to pan and flip every minute, ensuring even cooking without burning bread.
  5. Once sandwich browns and cheese has melted, remove from pan and serve immediately. Enjoy!
